About Us

Furnid Co., Ltd. is located in Jakarta City enjoying convenient transportation and beautiful environment. Having more than 12 years’ experience in the office furniture industry. We are specialized in office furniture. Our Factory has rich experience in the office furniture industry. Main products include office desk, office table, executive desk, staff desk, office workstation, computer desk, filing cabinet, meeting table, Hotel furniture etc. Besides, we are making great efforts to develop new products to meet different requirements. Our factory has own big production line, managing by a team of years experience leaders and professional designers. Adhering to the business principle of mutual benefits, we office furniture manufacturer have had a good reputation among our customers because of our perfect services, quality products and competitive prices.
